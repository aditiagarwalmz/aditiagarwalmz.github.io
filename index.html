The Road Taken!

18th Feb, 21

Who risk the future
Are mettlesome;
A lioness of a kin.
But cash and coins
Define wholesome
Can't keep a sane mind.

From two diverging roads in front,
I had to choose one.
I took the one most travelled by
that turned me into none.

O Captain! Me Captain!

18th Feb, 21

I found a phrase,
A thoughtful phrase
About how life goes on
A common meaning,
A common term
With fair words drawn.

I found a movie of my taste
And words mustn't go to waste
So I took it for meself
Now Captain has been my name
And so shall it remain.
